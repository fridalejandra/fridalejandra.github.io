I am a PhD candidate in geography at UCLA, where I also completed my undergraduate studies.
My research focuses on Antarctic sea ice variability and more boradly, Earth’s climate system. Currently, I am working on a project to better understand the timing of sea ice advance and retreat to serve as a point for climate model diagnostics. My previous work involved sea ice thickness variations around Antarctica.
